Childhood

I was born and raised in Kazakhstan, in a village called Chkalovo (Чкалово), located 300 km from the capital of the country Nur-Sułtan, in the north.

From an early age, I started writing poetry. The oldest poems I have kept are from 2001, I was 9 at the time. Until now I am writing in my native language - Russian.

Each of my parents made every effort to bring up the best qualities in me. Dad taught me to do all kinds of renovation works in the house and yard, to take care of domestic animals. Mom taught me to cook, sew. She showed me the beauty of literature. Already at the age of 15, I was delighted with Dostoevsky's depressive and very deep novels.

Study

At the age of 18, after graduating from high school, I successfully passed the entrance exams to study in Poland. After a year of "compensatory" study at the University of Rzeszów, I started my studies at the Warsaw School of Economics.

During my studies, I was very interested in football. I was a fan of FC Barcelona. I had the opportunity to meet the entire Polish national team during 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification.

In 2017, I graduated with a "very good" master's degree in Project Management.

I started working during my undergraduate studies. I earned my first money by writing studies for Russian philology students from my favorite Dostoyevsky's "The Karamazovy Brothers". For the first money I earned, I bought skates. (I can't ride them until now).
